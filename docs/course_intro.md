## Why this course?

- Practical skills are important to your education
- In Summer, you'll build software for a customer as a team in **Software Project**
- From previous iterations, many students lacked hands-on experience
- This course gives you a **technical base** to build upon

## Course structure

**10 labs** taking you from understanding to building:

1. Products, git, and your skill
2. Fix and deploy a backend
3. RESTful backend, testing & DB
4. AI integration
5. Telegram bot
6. Data and web
7. ML recommender
8. Flutter app
9. Agentic AI
10. Hackathon

<div class="note">* Subject to change</div>

---

## How to succeed

The course is designed to be **doable in the lab**.

- Attend the labs;
- Go through the tasks;
- Do extra if you have the skill;
- Ask questions from TAs, classmates, LLMs.

We want you to learn and apply on the go, this is the main skill.
